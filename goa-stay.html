⚠️ September Sea Advisory
Always check the beach flag before swimming. Do not swim if the red flag is raised — enforced by lifeguards. Green flag = safe to swim. Yellow = caution. Red = no swimming. Hotel pool is always a safe option.

⚠️ Safety & Health Tips in Goa
🚫 Never swim under red flag — rip currents are dangerous. Always check beach flags
💧 Drink only bottled water — avoid tap water, raw salads at street stalls
🦟 Use mosquito repellent in the evenings — especially near water
🛵 Slow down on scooters in rain — road surfaces get very slippery
💊 Carry ORS sachets — humidity causes rapid dehydration even without sun
📱 Save all emergency numbers offline — mobile data can be patchy in hills
🏥 Keep travel/health insurance details in phone photos
🔒 Hotel lockers available — leave valuables locked when on beach
